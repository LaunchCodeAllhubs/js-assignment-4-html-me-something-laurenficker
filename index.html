Grand Haven, Michigan

A Small Beach Town & Ocean-Like Views - Without Leaving the Midwest

Grand Haven State Park

Spend the day at Grand Haven State Park, lounging on the beach or walking the boardwalk to view the lighthouse.

Rosy Mound Natual Area

Get some sceneic hiking in at Rosy Mount Natural Area. Beware: there are lots of stairs - but the view is worth it!

Downtown Grand Haven

Enjoy restaurants, bars, shops, yard games, and more in Downtown Grand Haven.

Honorable Mentions:

    Odd Side Ales - for a beer flight or cocktails
    Kirby House - for a rooftop dinner with great pizza
    The Toasted Pickle - for the best sandwhiches around
    Sweet Temptations - for ice cream by the water

The Deck

Drive north 30 minutes to Muskegon to visit The Deck: the perfect vacation experience with great food, drinks, and live music - all on the beach.
